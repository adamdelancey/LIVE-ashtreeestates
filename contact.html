Why Ash Tree Estates?


You can benefit from my 24 years experience in Student Property Management.

Handling just a few exclusive, hand picked properties means you receive a professional, rapid and very personal service. I work from home and keep my Smart Phone on me 7 days a week, so you will always receive an immediate response.

You also have access to a loyal and trusted maintenance team. I have worked with them all for many years. One maintenance person also keeps keys and you will be given his/her contact details.

Located in Bristol, I am in close proximity to all the properties, so calling round to discuss issues is never a problem.

I am a member of the Property Redress Scheme, Client Money Protection Scheme and the Residential Landlords Association.

Tenants are not charged any fees for reserving my managed properties. Landlords are charged 10% of rent for full management.

Letting fee is 15% of one month’s rent and accompanied viewings are a further 25% of one month’s rent. However no new properties will be managed by Ash Tree Estates.

Enjoy looking through the site and I hope to hear from you soon!
